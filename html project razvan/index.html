The PHOENIX team is composed of dedicated students with a strong passion for robotics, programming, and innovation.

Our mission is to design and develop high-performance robots while actively sharing the knowledge and expertise we acquire.

About Us

Our team, Phoenix, was the 26th team to register for the FIRST Tech
Challenge Romania, earning the national number RO026. Internationally,
the team is registered under number 19079, which reminds us of the
intense level of competition among FTC teams every time we see it.

Each season, we strive to design a robot that surpasses the one before,
while upholding the core values of FIRST and spreading our passion for
robotics.

Looking ahead, our goals are to consistently reach new milestones,
strengthen our team management, and welcome new students who
will carry the torch for future generations.
